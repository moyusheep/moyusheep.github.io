About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering at Zhejiang University and University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My academic interests include medical imaging, computational imaging, machine learning, and signal processing.

My current work focuses on low-dose PET image reconstruction with deep unrolling methods, combining physical model-based reconstruction and deep learning.
