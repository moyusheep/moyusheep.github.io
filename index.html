About Me

I am interested in medical image reconstruction, computational imaging, deep learning, and model-based reconstruction algorithms. My current work focuses on low-dose PET image reconstruction with deep unrolling methods.

Research Interests

  • Low-dose PET reconstruction
  • Deep unrolling and model-based deep learning
  • Computational imaging and inverse problems
  • AI for healthcare
  • Signal processing

Research Experience

PET Image Reconstruction with Deep Unrolling

Developed a model-based deep learning framework for low-dose PET image reconstruction by combining EM update, U-Net-based regularization, and weighted fusion.

Hyperspectral-Assisted Lung Adenocarcinoma Cell Segmentation

Built a U-Net-based cell segmentation pipeline using pseudo-color pathology images synthesized from selected hyperspectral bands.

Virtual IHC Stain Transfer for Breast Cancer Biomarker Prediction

Developed a dual-stream generative framework for virtual stain transfer with pathology-aware feature learning and structure-preserving constraints.

Dynamic Control of Intelligent Metasurfaces

Explored FPGA-based bias-voltage array control and genetic-algorithm-based phase-pattern optimization for tunable metasurfaces.
